About Us

"Building a Secure Tomorrow, Today."
We are UNECSC, a student-run cybersecurity club at the University of New England.
Our mission is to promote cybersecurity awareness and provide valuable hands-on experience through hacking challenges, workshops, and projects.
Whether you're a student or a guest, you're welcome to explore and enhance your cyber defense skills with us.
